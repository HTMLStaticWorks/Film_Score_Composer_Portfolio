Reach Me
What I Offer

Every score starts
with a question

Before I write a single note, I need to understand what you want your audience to feel — not just hear. Here's how I work.

Process

How we work together

01

Discovery Call

30–45 min conversation about the project, its emotional goals, and what success looks like.

02

Spotting Session

We watch together and mark every moment where music enters, exits, and transforms.

03

Composition Rounds

Two rounds of revisions included. I share in-progress cues as I go — no surprises at delivery.

04

Final Delivery

Stems, mixed cues, and all master files. Music publishing and licensing handled transparently.

Services

What I compose

Film Scoring

Full scores for features, shorts, and documentaries. From single-instrument intimate pieces to full orchestral productions with session musicians. Rates from £4,000.

Game Audio

Adaptive music systems, looping ambiences, combat stingers, and cutscene scores. Delivered in Wwise-ready stems. Rates from £6,500.

Trailer Music

Cinematic trailer compositions optimised for impact, tension, and emotional release. Single-cue or full campaign. Rates from £1,200 per cue.

Electronic Score

Modular synthesis and hybrid electronic soundscapes for sci-fi, thriller, horror, and experimental projects. Rates from £3,200.

Sound Design

Music-to-sound-design continuums — where the score and the sound effects blur into something unified and immersive. Add-on to any project.

Score Review

One-hour session reviewing an existing score with feedback on emotional effectiveness, orchestration, and sync licensing readiness. £250/session.

Every project is priced individually

The rates above are starting points. Final pricing depends on project length, complexity, revision rounds, and licensing scope. I'll always send a detailed quote before any commitment.
